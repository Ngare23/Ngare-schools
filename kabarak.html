KABARAK UNIVERSITY

ABOUT

History

Kabarak University is a private institution of higher education in Kenya, established in 2002 by the second President of Kenya, H.E. Daniel Toroitich Arap Moi.

Location

The University is located in Nakuru County, about 20 km from Nakuru town and 12 km off the Nakuru-Eldama Ravine road

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

Academic programs in Kabarak University are spread across the following seven schools:

  • Education, humanities and social sciences
  • Science Engineering and technology
  • law
  • Business and Economics
  • Pharmacy
  • Medicine and Health sciences
  • Music and Media
